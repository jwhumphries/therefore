Welcome to **Therefore**, a space for philosophical and theological reflection.

## Why "Therefore"?

The word "therefore" marks the moment of conclusion, the point where premises meet their logical end. It's the bridge between thought and understanding.

{{quote author="Aristotle" source="Posterior Analytics"}}
All teaching and learning of an intellectual kind proceed from pre-existent knowledge.
{{/quote}}

## What to Expect

This blog will explore:

- **Philosophy of Mind** — consciousness, perception, and the nature of thought
- **Ethics** — virtue, duty, and the good life
- **Theology** — faith, reason, and transcendence
- **Metaphysics** — being, existence, and reality

Stay tuned for more posts exploring these timeless questions.
